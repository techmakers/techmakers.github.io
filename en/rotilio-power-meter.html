Rotilio Power Meter power is nothing without control

Accurately measure production and power consumption.

This product is the result of the cooperation between Techmakers and Consorzio Ingauno which built more than 200 PV plants. 

Technical info

  • App for the monitoring of PV plants
  • Up to 7 current clamps
  • Mixed single phase and three phase configurations
  • Active and reactive power measuring
  • OTA (Over-The-Air) firmware updates
  • Inverter alert monitoring for PV plants
  • Wi-fi or 3G communication
  • Expansion plate for customizations
