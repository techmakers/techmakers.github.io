IoT devices design and prototyping

One of our specialties is to be able to quickly realize prototypes of connected devices (IoT) and then to engineer their production, if necessary. We have already built devices for data collection in telemetry, with PLC connections on a serial port, control devices for the industry, energy meters integrated with the Web, Touch computers for the Internet of Things.

Integration of IoT devices in industrial machines, home automation

We make it possible to integrate with the WEB equipment and machinery, with only telemetry or even remote control. We therefore integrate existing equipment and machinery with our Rotilio PRO and Rotilio Maker electronic boards. In this way every object can be connected to the Internet in a short time, using secure data transmission channels.

Transfer of know-how to companies that want to accelerate the process of entry into the IoT market

We like to share knowledge with partners and end users, only in this way can solutions be born at the top of expectations, so we have prepared tutorials, courses and seminars on our products but also on the guiding concepts of the new technological frontier that companies will pass in the coming years.
