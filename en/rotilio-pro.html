Connect machines, systems and any kind of sensor to the Internet, both for data collection and remote controlling.

Industries are now venturing in the field of creating value or customers, end users, installers and engineers. Time-to-market and solution maintenance are top priorities.

The different communication ports and the sensors are connected to the outside world in real time through wi-fi, Ethernet and 3G connections. Because we developed a flexible communication protocol which does not need to start every time from scratch, we are fast and able to support many installations.
