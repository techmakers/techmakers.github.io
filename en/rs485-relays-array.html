Rotilio Relays Array turning on the lights

RS485 Relays Array

49,00€
net of VAT

You need a switch to turn anything on. This EC certified device includes eight remotely controllable switches, up to 1,200 meters.

By connecting up to 15 of such devices to a singleRotilio Pro or Rotilio Maker we can command 120 utilities with loads up to 15A, depending on the supply voltage.

  • Serial interface RS-485
  • Up to 1200 mt
  • Up to 128 controllable utilities
  • EC certified components
  • DIN rail mount option available
  • Detachable cable terminals

Load simulator for auxiliary power units, simulation workbench

Technical info

  • Board size (mm): 149x106x25 (WxDxH)
  • RS485 communication
  • Supply voltage: 12-24V
  • Current consumption at 12V: 600mA
  • Relays: 8
  • Permissible loads per relay: 12VDC/10A, 240VAC/7A, 12VDC/15A, 24VDC/15A, 125VAC/15A or 250VAC/10A
  • LED indicators for on relays
