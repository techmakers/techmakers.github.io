Rotilio Maker

Experiment and learn o develop IoT applications, a complete platform at your service.

We created Rotilio Maker as an answer to developers wanting to create their own IoT projects and looking for an exhaustive platform. Rotilio Maker gives you the tools to xplore the possibilities , and also to actually build prototypes which may become real products for the real market.

Rotilio Maker is a powerful and easy to use certificata CE development platform, which includes open source hardware and software, and is Arduino compatible.

Developers can start experimenting right away without the need to know about how sensors, relays and firmware work. They just need to look at simple javascript code examples available on GitHub.

Whether you are a Maker or not, if you are looking for a professional solution out of the box, Rotilio PRO is the answer, click here.

  • Rotilio.cc: ready to use interface for Rotilio Maker
  • Github: everything open source: code, app, firmware, circuit diagrams
  • Particle.io: technology that make Rotilio Maker work, all in your hands
